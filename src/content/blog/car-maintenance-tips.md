Driving in Timor-Leste presents unique challenges. Here are essential maintenance tips to keep your vehicle in top condition.

## 1. Regular Oil Changes

Change your engine oil every 5,000 km or as recommended by your manufacturer. Clean oil is vital for engine longevity.

## 2. Tire Maintenance

Check tire pressure weekly and inspect for wear. Properly inflated tires improve fuel economy and safety.

## 3. Brake Inspection

Have your brakes inspected regularly. Squeaking or grinding sounds indicate it's time for service.

## 4. Battery Care

Clean battery terminals and check connections. Extreme heat can shorten battery life.

## 5. Air Conditioning Service

Regular AC service ensures comfort in Timor-Leste's tropical climate.

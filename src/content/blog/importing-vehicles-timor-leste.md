Importing a vehicle to Timor-Leste can seem complex, but with the right partner, it's straightforward.

## Step 1: Choose Your Vehicle

Source from Japan, Singapore, or Australia through auctions or dealers.

## Step 2: Documentation

Key documents needed:

- Bill of lading
- Commercial invoice
- Certificate of origin
- Vehicle registration documents

## Step 3: Shipping & Insurance

Arrange shipping to Dili port with appropriate insurance coverage.

## Step 4: Customs Clearance

Our team handles all customs processes to ensure smooth clearance.

## Step 5: Registration

Complete local registration and licensing.

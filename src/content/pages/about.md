Founded with a vision to bring quality automotive services to Timor-Leste, Ashaz Autoz started as a small workshop and has grown into a comprehensive automotive service provider.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with suppliers in Japan, Singapore, and Australia, allowing us to offer a wide selection of quality vehicles at competitive prices.

Today, we are proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers across Dili and throughout Timor-Leste, providing everything from vehicle sales to professional workshop services.
